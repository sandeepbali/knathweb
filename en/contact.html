Reaching the Office

The office of Shri Kamal Nath is committed to maintaining an open and accessible channel of communication with the people of India, constituents of Chhindwara, party workers, media professionals, and all citizens who wish to engage with his office on matters of public interest, governance, and development.

With a distinguished career spanning over five decades in public service, Shri Kamal Nath has always believed that the strength of democracy lies in its ability to listen. Whether you are a constituent seeking assistance, a journalist covering parliamentary affairs, a community leader advocating for local development, or a citizen with a suggestion for national progress, his offices across Delhi, Bhopal, and Chhindwara are equipped and ready to assist you.

Each office serves a distinct purpose and handles specific types of queries. We encourage you to reach out to the most appropriate office based on your requirement. For constituency-related matters, the Chhindwara office is best suited. For state-level political affairs, the Bhopal office handles coordination. For parliamentary and national-level matters, the Delhi office serves as the primary point of contact.

Chhindwara District

Constituency Offices

Serving Chhindwara Since 1980

Chhindwara holds a special place in the political journey of Shri Kamal Nath, who has represented this constituency as a Member of Parliament for an unprecedented nine terms. The constituency offices across Chhindwara district are designed to ensure that every citizen has access to representation and assistance, regardless of where they reside within the district.

The main constituency office at Congress Bhawan in Chhindwara city serves as the central coordination point. This office is staffed by experienced personnel who are well-versed in handling a wide range of issues, from individual grievances and development project queries to government scheme enrollment assistance and community welfare initiatives.

Constituents can visit the office during regular working hours without prior appointment for general inquiries. However, for matters requiring detailed discussion or meeting with senior staff members, scheduling an appointment in advance is recommended. The office also organizes periodic public interaction sessions where constituents can directly present their concerns.

How to Reach the Chhindwara Office

Chhindwara is well-connected by road from major cities in Madhya Pradesh. The Congress Bhawan is centrally located in Chhindwara city and is easily accessible by auto-rickshaw or local transport from the bus stand and railway station. The nearest airport is Nagpur (approximately 130 km), followed by Bhopal (approximately 260 km).

Matters Handled

  • Public grievance redressal and follow-up with government departments
  • Assistance with central and state government welfare scheme enrolment
  • Development project monitoring and coordination
  • Community welfare and social development initiatives
  • Educational scholarship guidance and support

Main Constituency Office

Address: Congress Bhawan, Chhindwara, Madhya Pradesh

The office welcomes walk-in visitors during working hours for general inquiries. For specific appointments or detailed discussions, please contact the office in advance to schedule a suitable time.

District Coordination

Beyond the main office, Shri Kamal Nath's team maintains an extensive network of coordinators across all tehsils and major villages in Chhindwara district. These coordinators serve as the first point of contact for residents in remote areas, ensuring that distance does not become a barrier to accessing representation and assistance.

Local coordinators can help relay urgent matters, collect grievance applications, and provide initial guidance on available government schemes and welfare programs. They also assist in organizing local meetings and public interaction sessions.

Written Representations

Constituents may also submit written representations at the Congress Bhawan office. Written submissions should include complete details of the matter, the applicant's full name, address, contact number, and any relevant supporting documents.

All written representations are logged, acknowledged, and forwarded to the appropriate department for action. Follow-up updates are provided to the applicant through phone or in person.

Press & Media

Media Inquiries

Press Office

The media relations team of Shri Kamal Nath's office is dedicated to ensuring transparent and timely communication with members of the press and media fraternity. The press office handles all media inquiries, interview requests, statement clarifications, and press conference coordination.

Journalists and media professionals seeking official comments, press statements, or interview appointments are requested to direct their inquiries through the official channels listed here. The media coordination team endeavors to respond to all legitimate press queries within 24 hours during working days.

Press Accreditation & Coverage

Media personnel wishing to cover events, rallies, or official engagements of Shri Kamal Nath are advised to seek prior accreditation through the press office. This helps in ensuring smooth logistics and appropriate seating/positioning arrangements for media coverage. Accreditation requests should be submitted at least 48 hours before the event, wherever possible.

Official Statements & Press Releases

Official press releases, statements, and media briefs are shared through the office's verified social media channels and directly with accredited journalists. For authenticity, please verify all statements through the official Twitter handle @OfficeOfKNath before publication.
